I have taken inspiration from mainly five websites. I have compared the websites to mine. They are listed below:

1. Homepage






I have taken inspiration from this template posted in the colorlib. In the template, there is a box that shows the basic info of the person in a box in his porfolio which is centered. I have incorporated the design in my home page showing my current educational status with facebook page linked as well.

2. Navbar in Contact Page



As we can clearly see in the pictures. They look similar but are different. The one on the left is the social media bar on the left. It's padding increases when we hover. But the nav bar of my contact page has no such padding when we hover. The current page is highlighted by coloring the text yellow in nav bar. And the nav bar is located on the right compare to the social media bar which is located on the left.

3. Navbar in Blog Page






The navbar of my blog page is inspired from the navbar of the thoughtbot page. I have kept icons as well for the the users to easily understand and navigate. I have tried to make the navbar simple and minimalist.

4. Contact Page



As we can clearly see in the pictures. They look similar but are different. The one on the left is facebook login page. The textbox are bigger. With placeholder being the thing you have to enter. I on the other hand, have not made the textbox bigger. I have hiiden its boder and made the border of the div that it is in and the label of it visible with rounded corners. I have also added a range slider so that the user can easily rate the design of my web pages.

5. Footer





The footers of my pages are inspired from the footer of the GEMS home page. But I feel that the font size of the GEMS home page is too small which makes reading difficult. In my footer, I have used a larger font size which is centered and when clicked takes to the facebook page for contact. But due to privacy reason I have linked to facebook login page.
